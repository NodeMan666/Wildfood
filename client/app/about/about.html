ABOUT US

wildfood.in is a plant community, where gardeners, foragers, growers, restaurants, chefs and bush crafters can locate plants, share knowledge and find resources related to useful, edible and medicinal plant species.

The platform will facilitate real world discovery, identification and learning, while fostering a network of producers, suppliers and consumers to promote and exchange plants, seeds, food and services.

If you have any questions about our website, you can contact us or just take a tour to discover more about wild food. Enjoy!

MISSION

To nurture and support an online community around useful plants with discussion forums and resources, providing locally relevant and accurate information via a friendly and intuitive web-based application.
